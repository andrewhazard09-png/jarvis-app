Here's a factual summary of current knowledge about sports:

Sports are physical activities organized and played competitively, with numerous types, including team sports, individual sports, and adventure sports. The International Olympic Committee (IOC) recognizes over 33 Olympic sports, while various other international and national governing bodies oversee a broader range of sports disciplines. Advances in fitness technology, sports analytics, and athlete safety have transformed the sports landscape, enhancing competition and promoting athlete well-being. The increasing popularity of sports has spawned a multi-billion-dollar industry, driven by global sponsorships, broadcasting, and merchandise sales.
