Topic: news

Current knowledge about news includes its rapid evolution with the advent of the internet and social media, which have transformed the way news is produced, disseminated, and consumed. Traditional news sources such as newspapers and television have been supplemented by online news outlets, bloggers, and social media influencers. According to the Reuters Institute Digital News Report, the majority of people worldwide now get their news from online sources, and many are increasingly relying on social media platforms to stay informed.
