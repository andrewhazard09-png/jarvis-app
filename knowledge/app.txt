Topic: app
Last updated: 2026-03-23T04:29:31.125851

An app, or application, is a software program designed to perform a specific task on a mobile device, computer, or other electronic device. Apps provide users with an interface to access various features, functions, or services, often through a touchscreen or user interface. They can be free or paid, and many are downloadable from app stores such as Apple's App Store and Google Play. The use of apps has become ubiquitous in modern life, with millions of apps available for a wide range of purposes, from social media and entertainment to productivity and education. Today, apps continue to evolve and are driving the development of emerging technologies like augmented reality and the Internet of Things (IoT).
