Topic: game
Last updated: 2026-03-23T06:59:46.324574

A game is a structured form of play or competition with rules, objectives, and outcomes. Games have been a part of human culture since ancient times, serving as a means of entertainment, socialization, and cognitive development. Modern games encompass a wide range of forms, including board games, card games, video games, and outdoor activities. The study of games, known as ludology, has gained recognition as a distinct field of research within the humanities.
